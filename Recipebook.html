Grilled Chesse

  • Time: 3 mins
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Cheddear chesse 2 slices
Bread 2 slices
Butter 1 table spoon

Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
Step 2: Put the cheese on the bread.
Step 3: Add on 1 tsp of butter on the pan
Step 4: Put both slices of bread on the pan and grill it for 2 minutes.
Step 5: Enjoy

Hot Dog

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 1 people
Ingredients Quantity
Hot Dog 1 hot dog
Bun 1 bun
Condiments

Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
Step 2: Set the grill for 155 degrees .
Step 3: Put on the hot dog
Step 4: When the hot dog is cooked, put it in the bun
Step 5: Enjoy

Sandwich

  • Time: 5 mins
  • Serves: 1 people
Ingredients Quantity
Ham As many as you like
Bread 2 Slices
Cheese 2 Slice
Condiments

Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
Step 2: Take out 2 pieces of bread.
Step 3: Put on as many pieces of ham as you want. (don't put too much)
Step 4: Put on cheese and condiments on it.
Step 5: Enjoy
